# Bright-TV Case Study
## Overview
BrightTV is a subscription-based streaming service seeking to grow its subscriber base during the current financial year. The company's CEO has identified subscriber growth as the primary strategic objective
and has engaged a data analyst to provide actionable insights that will support the Customer Value Management (CVM) team in achieving this goal.
## Objective
Grow BrightTV's subscription base for the current financial year.
| Focus Area | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Subscriber Acquisition | Identifying characteristics of high-value new subscribers to inform targeting strategies |
| Subscriber Retention | Understanding churn signals and behaviour patterns of subscribers at risk of cancelling |
| Subscriber Re-engagement | Profiling lapsed subscribers who may be won back through targeted campaigns |
